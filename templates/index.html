Welcome.

This website aggregates the blogs of graduate students at George Mason University's Department of Computational Social Science, part of the The Krasnow Institute for Advanced Study. As this department name implies, we study and write about CSS. It's a bit of a free-wheeling discipline, spanning anthropology, sociology, psychology, economics, and computer science. Mostly, we ABMs, simulating social or economic systems, hoping to discover how they tick.

Only excerpts exist on this website. Once you click a link, you'll visit the student's own blog page. If you'd like to follow our work without visiting this page, subscribe to our RSS feed or follow our twitter bot.
